Akan Name Finder

Many tribes in Africa have a way of naming their children. For some, it is according to the time of day they were born, others depending on how the whether was during their time of birth. The Akan, a tribe in Ghana, name their children based on what day they were born. The name is usually different depending on the gender of the child.

This app is to help you find your Akan name. Just enter your birthday and gender and we will let you know your Akan name.
